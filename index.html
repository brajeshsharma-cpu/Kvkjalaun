About KVK Jalaun

Govt. Agriculture Farm, Rura Mallu, PO Shahjadpura, Jalaun — ICAR · BUAT Banda

Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Rura Mallu, Jalaun has been performing a considerable and commendable job of rural development in district Jalaun since its very inception in 2005 — through multifarious activities like Training, Demonstration, On Farm Testing (OFTs) and Farm Advisory Services.

Jalaun district falls in Jhansi Division and is one of the 75 districts of Uttar Pradesh. The northern boundary is naturally demarcated by river Yamuna, while the western boundary adjoins Madhya Pradesh. The district is divided into 5 Tehsils — Orai, Jalaun, Kalpi, Madhogarh and Konch — and 9 Development Blocks: Rampura, Kuthond, Madhogarh, Konch, Jalaun, Nadigaon, Dakore, Mahewa and Kadaura.

KVK Jalaun actively contributes in organic farming, sustainable crop production, seed production, flower cultivation, spice cultivation, vegetable cultivation, fruit production and allied areas. It has established strong collaboration with line departments — State Agriculture, DRDA, Horticulture and others.

ICAR Projects & Schemes

National programmes implemented by KVK Jalaun for district farmers

NFSM

National Food Security Mission

Boosting production of wheat, pulses and coarse cereals through improved agronomic practices and quality inputs across Jalaun district.

 NMOOP

National Mission on Oilseeds & Oil Palm

Expanding sesamum and mustard cultivation — major Kharif and Rabi oilseed crops — to strengthen edible oil production in the district.

 PPVFRA

Plant Variety Protection

Protecting farmers' rights and encouraging conservation of locally-adapted and traditional plant varieties in Jalaun district.

 OFTs

On Farm Trials

Technology validation under actual farming conditions across Jalaun's 9 development blocks to guide location-specific recommendations.

 FLDs

Front Line Demonstrations

Large-scale demonstrations on farmers' fields bridging the yield gap between research potential and actual farm productivity.

 SKD

Skill Development

Vocational training for rural youth in agri-enterprise, food processing, mushroom cultivation, vermi-composting and allied occupations.
