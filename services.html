Services

KVK Jalaun — Jalaun district, Uttar Pradesh

This section covers KVK Jalaun's work in Services for the farming communities of Jalaun district. KVK Jalaun has been serving Jalaun since 2005 under ICAR and BUAT Banda, conducting training, demonstrations, on-farm trials and advisory across all 9 development blocks — Rampura, Kuthond, Madhogarh, Konch, Jalaun, Nadigaon, Dakore, Mahewa and Kadaura.

For detailed information about this programme, reports or to request advisory support, please contact KVK Jalaun directly at Govt. Agriculture Farm, Rura Mallu, PO Shahjadpura, Distt. Jalaun (UP) – 285 001, or call the Kisan Helpline: 1800-180-1551.
