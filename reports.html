KVK Jalaun Reports

Official ICAR-mandated progress reporting

KVK Jalaun submits regular progress reports to ICAR as part of its mandate. These reports document all training programmes, demonstrations, on-farm trials, advisory services, and technology transfer activities conducted during the reporting period.

📊

Annual Progress Report (APR)

Comprehensive yearly report covering all KVK activities — training conducted, demonstrations laid, OFTs, advisory services, publications and impact assessment. Submitted annually to ICAR.

Access APR Portal →
📈

Quarterly Progress Report (QPR)

Quarter-wise progress reports documenting training conducted, demonstrations established, OFTs, advisory services rendered and other activities. Submitted four times a year.

Access QPR Portal →
📋

Monthly Progress Report (MPR)

Monthly reports capturing day-to-day activities, farmer contacts, advisory services rendered, field visits and other ongoing KVK Jalaun activities throughout the year.

QRT Reports

Quarterly Review Team reports providing external expert assessment of KVK Jalaun activities, achievements and recommendations for programme improvement and focus areas.

Annual Action Plan (AAP)

The Annual Action Plan outlines KVK Jalaun's planned training programmes, on-farm trials, front line demonstrations, advisory activities and priority focus areas for each year — aligned with ICAR mandates and district agricultural priorities. The AAP is prepared at the start of each year in consultation with scientists and stakeholders.
