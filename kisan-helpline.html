Toll-free agricultural helpline and advisory services for farmers of Jalaun district

Kisan Call Centre

Expert guidance at your fingertips — free of charge

TOLL FREE KISAN HELPLINE
1800-180-1551
Monday to Saturday  ·  No charges

The Kisan Call Centre is a 24×7 service that connects farmers with agricultural experts. Farmers of Jalaun district can call the toll-free number to get guidance on any farm-related issue — from crop diseases to soil problems, from livestock health to market prices.

KVK Jalaun also provides direct advisory services through its campus — farmers can visit the centre at Rura Mallu for consultation with Subject Matter Specialists in agronomy, horticulture, animal science, plant protection and soil science.
