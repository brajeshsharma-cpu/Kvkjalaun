Our Team

Scientists and Subject Matter Specialists of KVK Jalaun

KVK Jalaun's multidisciplinary team of scientists brings together expertise in agronomy, horticulture, animal science, plant protection and soil science to deliver comprehensive support to Jalaun district farmers. Despite working with limited staff since its inception in 2005, the team has achieved commendable results in technology transfer, capacity building and farm advisory.

Featured at Doordarshan Kisan — 22 September 2023

👨‍🌾

Dr Vister Joshi

Subject Matter Specialist — KVK Jalaun

Dr Vister Joshi from KVK Jalaun delivered his expert views on Agricultural Start-ups at Doordarshan Kisan on 22nd September 2023, encouraging rural youth of Jalaun district to take up agri-entrepreneurship as a viable livelihood option. This appearance highlighted KVK Jalaun's commitment to promoting agri-enterprise beyond traditional farming.
