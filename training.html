Training Programmes

Hands-on capacity building for farmers, farm women and rural youth of Jalaun district

Training at KVK Jalaun

Building agricultural knowledge and skills across Jalaun district

Training is a core mandate activity of KVK Jalaun. The centre conducts a wide range of on-campus and off-campus training programmes for farmers, farm women, rural youth and extension workers — covering all major aspects of crop production, livestock management, post-harvest technology and agri-entrepreneurship.

👨‍🌾

Farmers Training (On-campus)

Residential training programmes at KVK campus covering soil health, seed treatment, improved agronomic practices, pest and disease management, and water management for major crops.

🚜

Off-campus Training

Training at village level — as recently conducted at Village Khajuri — bringing expert guidance directly to farmers' doorstep across all 9 development blocks of Jalaun.

👩‍🌾

Farm Women Training

Specialised programmes for rural women in kitchen gardening, food processing, mushroom cultivation, vermi-composting and other income-generating activities.

🧑‍💼

Rural Youth & Entrepreneurship

Vocational training for rural youth in agri-enterprise, organic farming, seed production, spice processing and other farm-based business opportunities.

🐄

Livestock Management

Training in scientific cattle and buffalo management, breed improvement, dairy farming, goat rearing, poultry and fodder production for Jalaun's livestock farming community.

🌿

Organic Farming

Hands-on training in organic crop production methods, bio-fertilizer preparation, integrated pest management, vermi-composting and sustainable farming practices.

🌻

Horticulture & Allied

Training in vegetable cultivation, fruit production, flower growing, spice cultivation and post-harvest handling of horticultural produce.

🧪

Soil Health Management

Soil testing interpretation, soil health card follow-up, balanced fertilizer use, micronutrient management and soil organic matter improvement for Jalaun's soils.

💡

Skill Development Programmes

ICAR-sponsored skill development training in food processing, agri-business management, value chain development and rural entrepreneurship for unemployed youth.

Recent Training Activity

Off-campus training at Village Khajuri

Off Campus Training Programme

KVK Jalaun recently conducted an off-campus training programme at Village Khajuri focusing on improved Kharif crop production. The programme covered seed treatment, integrated nutrient management, pest and disease management, and water-saving irrigation techniques.

Off-campus trainings bring KVK expertise directly to the village level, making it accessible to farmers who cannot travel to the KVK campus — a key part of our outreach strategy across Jalaun's 9 development blocks.
