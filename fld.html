Front Line Demonstrations (FLDs)

Showcasing improved technologies directly on farmers' fields across all 9 development blocks of Jalaun

What are FLDs?

Bridging the gap between research and farmers' fields

Front Line Demonstrations (FLDs) are conducted on farmers' fields to demonstrate the performance of improved technologies — new varieties, better agronomic practices, integrated crop management — under actual field conditions. KVK Jalaun conducts FLDs across all major crops of Jalaun district, helping farmers see the difference improved technology makes on their own land.

CFLD — Oilseeds Programme

Cluster Front Line Demonstrations for oilseed crops

Under the Cluster Front Line Demonstration (CFLD) programme for oilseeds, KVK Jalaun has recently distributed certified seed of improved oilseed varieties to farmers across multiple development blocks of Jalaun district.

The CFLD programme focuses on sesamum (Kharif) and mustard (Rabi) — the two primary oilseed crops of the district — demonstrating improved varieties and production practices to reduce the yield gap and increase oilseed production in Jalaun.
