District Profile — Jalaun

Agricultural, geographical and demographic profile of Jalaun district, Uttar Pradesh

Jalaun District at a Glance

Jhansi Division, Uttar Pradesh

Jalaun district is one of the 75 districts of Uttar Pradesh, falling in Jhansi Division. The northern boundary of the district is naturally demarcated by river Yamuna, while the western boundary is demarcated by Madhya Pradesh.

Agricultural Potential

Vast resource base for development in Jalaun

3.45 L ha

Cultivated Land

Of the 4.56 lakh ha total geographical area, 3.451 lakh ha is under cultivation — reflecting the strong agricultural character of Jalaun district and vast potential for productivity improvement.

115%

Cropping Intensity

A cropping intensity of 115% indicates good coverage of multiple cropping seasons and significant potential for further intensification through improved varieties and irrigation expansion.

942

Revenue Villages

With 942 revenue villages across 9 development blocks, Jalaun has a large and geographically spread rural farming community — the primary beneficiary of KVK Jalaun's technology transfer activities.
